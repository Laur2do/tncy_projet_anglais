How to play Slam Learning

It is very simple to start playing Slam Learning:

  1. Select one or more word decks you want to learn
    Alternatively and additionally, you can also select your own Words decks and questions decks as CSV files.
  2. You can start the game!

Slam Learning basics

The goal of the game is to completely reveal the generated grid. For this, you will have two repeated steps:

  1. Guess a letter
    • You need to answer a question to find some unrevealed letter in the grid
    • If you answer is wrong, you will restart to step A.
    • If you answer is correct, you will have the explanation and go to the next step.
  2. Pick and guess a word
    • You can now click on any word you like on the grid to select it.
      Be careful not to select a letter common to two words: the game can't know the one you meant!
    • Once a word is selected, you will have a definition to help you find the correct answer.
    • If you guess right, the word will be revealed on the grid.
    • Either way, you will restart to step A.
